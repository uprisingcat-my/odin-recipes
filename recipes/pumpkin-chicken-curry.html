Pumpkin Chicken Curry

Description

An attempt to make a minimal curry with the ingredients at hand that would produce enough gravy to support 2-3 rice meals.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin ~ 300g
  • Chicken ~ 150g
  • Curry powder ~ 2 tsp
  • Turmeric ~ 1/2 tsp
  • Chilli powder ~ 1/2 tsp
  • Coconut milk powder ~ 2.5 tsp
  • Onion ~ 1
  • Garlic cloves ~ 3
  • Salt
  • Dark soy sauce ~ 1 tsp
  • Ketchup ~ 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Cut pumpkin into roughly 2-3 cm cubes.
  2. Then chop onion and garlic.
  3. Mix the coconut milk powder in about 100ml of warm water.
  4. Add 2 tsp of cooking oil to the pan and start frying onion till they becomes soft in low heat, then add chopped garlic.
  5. Add the spices and mix well for about 30 seconds.
  6. Now add checken and cook them for 2-3 minutes in medium heat.
  7. After that, add pumpkin, 2.5 cups of water, soy sauce, ketchup and liing it to a boil. Then simmer it for about 25 minutes.
  8. Finally, add coconut milk mixture and cook for another 2 minutes and add salt if necessary.
